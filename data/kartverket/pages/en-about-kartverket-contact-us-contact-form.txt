Contact us
You can use this form to send us general questions about property registration, or if you have questions about our services. Once your inquiry has been received, you will be assigned a case number and a copy of your submission will be sent to you by email.
Privacy
Personal information that you fill out in the form will not be used for other purposes. They are stored on the Norwegian Mapping Authority's servers, and are deleted in accordance with fixed routines.
[Read more about data protection policy in this article.](https://www.kartverket.no/en/about-kartverket/data-protection-policy)
Public records
Please note that all inquiries to the Norwegian Mapping Authority are registered and archived, and may appear in the public record journal.
