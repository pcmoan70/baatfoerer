Contact us

You can use this form to send us general questions about property registration, or if you have questions about our services. Once your inquiry has been received, you will be assigned a case number and a copy of your submission will be sent to you by email.

Privacy

Personal information that you fill out in the form will not be used for other purposes. They are stored on the Norwegian Mapping Authority's servers, and are deleted in accordance with fixed routines.

Read more about data protection policy in this article.

Public records

Please note that all inquiries to the Norwegian Mapping Authority are registered and archived, and may appear in the public record journal.

More information about public record journal

The Norwegian Mapping Authority is required to handle all documents, including electronic inquiries, in accordance with the Freedom of Information Act (Act of 19 May 2006 No. 16). This means, among other things, that all case documents, including correspondence to and from the Norwegian Mapping Authority, are generally public. Anyone may request access to such documents under the Act. Therefore, do not send inquiries containing information you do not wish to be made public.

If a document contains confidential information, such information will be redacted before access is granted. Information concerning a person’s state of health, or that someone is or has been a patient, is always considered confidential.

Under the Archives Act (Act of 4 December 1992 No. 126), the Norwegian Mapping Authority is obliged to maintain a public record journal. This journal lists all correspondence to and from the Norwegian Mapping Authority. As a general rule, the following information appears in the public record journal: the sender’s name, the recipient’s name, the date of the document, and the subject title of the inquiry. The journal is always kept in such a way that confidential information is not disclosed.

Tinglysing og eiendomsinformasjon

Velg dette om du har spørsmål til hvordan du skal tinglyse noe på en eiendom. Hvis saken gjelder en spesifikk eiendom, trenger vi å vite hvilken kommune eiendommen ligger i, samt gårds- og bruksnummer. Vi kan kun gi generell veiledning på alle henvendelser vi mottar.

Ønsker du informasjon om hva som er tinglyst på en eiendom, hvem som er tinglyst eier eller for å se om noe er blitt tinglyst, kan du selv finne informasjonen på Eiendomsregisteret

Kan jeg sende dokumenter for tinglysing her?

Nei. Dokumenter som skal tinglyses må sendes inn per post. Det er ikke mulig å sende dem inn via kontaktskjema eller e-post. Vi kan heller ikke forhåndsvurdere dokumenter som skal tinglyses.

Tinglysing og eigedomsinformasjon

Vel dette om du har spørsmål om korleis du skal tinglyse noko på ein eigedom. Dersom saka gjeld ein spesifikk eigedom, treng vi å vite kva for kommune eigedomen ligg i, samt gards- og bruksnummer. Vi kan berre gi generell rettleiing på alle førespurnadar vi mottek.

Ønskjer du informasjon om kva som er tinglyst på ein eigedom, kven som er tinglyst som eigar, eller for å sjå om noko er blitt tinglyst, kan du sjølv finne informasjonen på Eiendomsregisteret.

Kan eg sende dokument for tinglysing her?

Nei. Dokument som skal tinglysast, må sendast inn per post. Det er ikkje mogleg å sende dei inn via kontaktskjema eller e-post. Vi kan heller ikkje gje ei førehandsvurdering av dokument som skal tinglysast.

Property and ownership registration

Select this option if you have questions about how to register something on a property. Please note that we can only provide general guidance.

If you want information about what is registered on a property, who is the registered owner, or to check if something has been registered, you can also call our customer center on +47 32 11 80 00.

Can I send documents for registration here?

Documents to be registered must be sent by post. It is not possible to submit them via the contact form or email. Additionally, we cannot pre-assess documents for registration.

Self-registration of property details

Select this option if you have questions about our new service Self-registration.

In this service, owners and leaseholders can register selected information themselves. Initially, this applies to certain details concerning residential and holiday home units.

The registration itself is completed on the website, Eiendomsregisteret, where you will also find guidance on how to register and other relevant information.

Read more about self-registration of building information.

Informasjon om opplasting av vedlegg

Information about uploading attachements

You may upload up to three attachments in PDF, PNG or JPG format, with a maximum file size of 10 MB per attachment. If the attachment is over 10 MB, your request must be sent as an email to post@kartverket.no.

Please do not include personal identification numbers or other sensitive information for privacy reasons.
