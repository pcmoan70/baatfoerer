Båtførerprøven tas på et av Norsk Test sine testsentre.

The Norwegian Boating License must be taken at one of Norsk Test's certified test centers.
Search by name, city or zipcode in the search-field to find a test center near you.
You must contact the test center yourself to schedule the exam.
